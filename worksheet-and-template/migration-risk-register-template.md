# Migration Risk Register Template

A risk register is not pessimism. It is how you protect your timeline and launch confidence by making risks visible early.

Use this template to track risks, triggers, owners, and mitigation plans.

#### Risk register template

| Risk ID | Risk description                            | Category | Probability | Impact | Mitigation plan                       | Trigger                      | Owner             | Status |
| ------- | ------------------------------------------- | -------- | ----------- | ------ | ------------------------------------- | ---------------------------- | ----------------- | ------ |
| R-001   | Variant mapping changes purchase behavior   | Data     | Medium      | High   | Validate complex products early       | Demo shows incorrect options | Technical Lead    | Open   |
| R-002   | Missing redirect coverage for priority URLs | SEO      | Medium      | High   | Build redirect worksheet and validate | 404 spike on top URLs        | Marketing and SEO | Open   |
| R-003   | Plugin or custom data not preserved         | Data     | Medium      | High   | Define Custom Job requirements        | Demo reveals missing fields  | Project Manager   | Open   |
| R-004   | Final sync timing misaligned with cutover   | Launch   | Low         | High   | Plan final sync window and sign-off   | Orders missing after launch  | PM                | Open   |
| R-005   | Team lacks time to validate properly        | Delivery | Medium      | High   | Use Managed support and structured QA | Validation backlog grows     | Business Owner    | Open   |

Suggested categories:

* Data integrity
* SEO continuity
* Integrations and operations
* Timeline and coordination
* Scope creep
* Compliance and privacy

#### How to use the risk register

* Review weekly during planning
* Review daily during the final week
* Close risks only when mitigation is completed or acceptance is documented
