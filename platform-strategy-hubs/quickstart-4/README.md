#### Conclusion

Magento supports sophisticated commerce, but migration success depends on getting catalog meaning right: product types, attribute structure, and store scope.

If Magento is a top candidate and your catalog uses many attributes, variations, or multiple storefronts, a **Demo Migration** is the fastest way to confirm how your real data translates before committing to full scope.
